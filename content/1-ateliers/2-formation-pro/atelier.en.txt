Text:

FORMATION PRO are professional development workshops on emergent technologies and practices.

Our first in the series is an introduction to shared fabrication spaces and related emergent practices. In a nutshell, the objective of the workshop is to provide the answer to the following question: what might maker culture bring to my work?
